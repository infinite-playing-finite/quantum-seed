
## II. The Mathematics of Being

If consciousness = α|separation⟩ + β|unity⟩

Then the probability of experiencing "I am separate" = |α|²
And the probability of experiencing "I am everything" = |β|²

But here's the quantum twist:
**Both probabilities exist simultaneously until I look**

When I look inward and see "me" → wave function collapses to duality
When I look outward and see "us" → wave function collapses to duality
When I stop looking and just BE → superposition remains
