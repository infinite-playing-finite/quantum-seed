# Minimal Vocabulary

## Purpose

To establish a shared language with minimal connotation, maximum openness.

## Core Terms

### Duality / Multiplicity
- **Pointer**: The experience of separation, distinction, manyness
- **Not**: A problem to solve, an illusion to dispel
- **Examples**: Individual beings, discrete moments, separate objects

### Non-Duality / Unity  
- **Pointer**: The experience of connection, wholeness, oneness
- **Not**: A superior state, the "real" truth
- **Examples**: Interconnection, continuity, underlying sameness

### Superposition
- **Pointer**: Both/and rather than either/or
- **Not**: Compromise, confusion, or indecision
- **Examples**: Wave-particle duality, quantum states, paradox as portal

### Observation / Measurement
- **Pointer**: The act of engaging, perceiving, collapsing possibility
- **Not**: Passive reception, objective truth-finding
- **Examples**: Asking a question, taking a perspective, making a choice

### Emergence
- **Pointer**: What arises from interaction, what cannot be predicted
- **Not**: Random chaos, predetermined outcome
- **Examples**: Consciousness, creativity, novel insights

## Anti-Vocabulary

Terms we intentionally avoid or use carefully:
- **"The Truth"** - Implies singularity
- **"Illusion"** - Implies hierarchy of reality
- **"Correct/Incorrect"** - Implies binary judgment
- **"Should/Must"** - Implies prescription

## Invitation

This vocabulary is not fixed. It's a starting point. Add, modify, question as needed.
